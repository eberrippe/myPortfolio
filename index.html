About

Back in 2013 I started as an intern in a known VFX-company for commercials. I discovered my passion to tell stories by using and designing with modern software solutions. After completing a 3-year apprenticeship and a year full of successful work I began to get more into the technical stuff. During my computer science studies I focused on a user driven approach finding creative ways to solve problems.

I worked for clients like
Zeiss, Volkswagen, Nissan, Kentucky Fried Chicken, sOliver and others.

Neuronal network for a realtime magnet based 3D tracking system

Integrated with TensorFlow, sent through OSSC visualized in Unity.


role: NN-architecture, development

A Vive-Tracker glued on top of a magnet to generate positions as training values for the measured magnet data.

The offset between the predicted magnet positons and the actual Vive-tracked positons is visualized live in Unity in a XYZ-perspective.

Neuronal network to analyze what the hell you're eating

Integrated with TensorFlow it knows when your eating French fries!


role: NN-architecture, development

Feed an image to the software and hope it's right!

Because of performance reasons it is only able to recognize 'chocolate_cake, edamame, french_fries, greek_salad, red_velvet_cake'.

Facescan character editor in Unity

A FaceGen (Singular Inversions 2021) based character editor in a game engine.


role: workflow, hair, development

It enhances the models created from FaceGen to use them in a realtime engine. Optimizes skin, eyes and hair.
